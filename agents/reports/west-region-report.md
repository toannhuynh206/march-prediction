## 2. Team-by-Team Profiles

## 8. Summary & Recommendations to Team Lead

### Key Findings:
1. **Florida is the clear West Region favorite** -- +36.24 KenPom rating is ~+8 over the field. Assign 35-45% of brackets to Florida winning the region.
2. **The West has 4 volatile first-round games** (Memphis/CSU, Missouri/Drake, Kansas/Arkansas, UConn/Oklahoma) -- more than typical. Set regional chaos expectation high.

6. **Apply blue-blood deflator to Kansas futures** per D003 -- this is a weak 21-12 KU team, and the market line reflects it (only -4.5 vs a 10-seed).
7. **Arkansas is the most dangerous double-digit seed** -- Calipari's 59 career tournament wins + Fland returning from injury + late-season surge = high upset potential through multiple rounds.
