## 1. Region Overview

The 2025 Midwest Region is headlined by **No. 1 Houston**, widely considered the most dominant defensive team in the country. The region features several blue-blood programs (Kentucky, Purdue, Gonzaga) and dangerous mid-majors (McNeese, High Point). Houston's path to the Final Four appears relatively clear, but the bottom half of the bracket (Tennessee-Kentucky corridor) is loaded with talent.
